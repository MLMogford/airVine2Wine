About Us

Air Vine2Wine is a new enterprose connecting Grape Growers, Wine Makers and Wine Lovers.



Wine Lovers
Suitable for Individuals, Wine Clubs, Corporate customers who want to celebrate an occasion
The accoiunt holder not only recieves a personalised product but can choose to be an essential part of it's growth and development on it's journey from vine to wine.
This service personalises your any celebration by offering customisable wine including:

  • Grape selection
  • Personal preferences for cultivation including pruning and watering
  • Small batch wine making with your selection of yeast and fermentation level
  • Botle type selection and custom labelling

Experienced viticulturaluists are available to assist with pruning selection and recommendation Expert wine makers will ensure the best advice on the options available tailored to your taste We assist in designing your labels and encourage inclusive and personal designs.

Wine Makers
This service guarantees customers for your excess wine vessel capacity, expand your sproduction without worrying about finding customers once the wine is made

Grape Growers
Ensure the resources you put into your vines doesn't go to waste. Surplus grapes can be matched with wine makers.
